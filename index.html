Quality Control

ALF’s commitment to quality is further validated by its adherence to ISO 9001:2015 standards, ensuring that every lubricant from synthetic engine oils to industrial grease meets the specific performance tiers required by API and ACEA specifications. Batch retention samples are archived for longitudinal stability monitoring, providing full traceability throughout the product lifecycle.

About ALF

ALMANYA LUBRICANTS FACTORY (ALF) is an advanced manufacturing facility situated in the Amriya Public Free Zone, featuring a $4 million turnkey plant engineered to strict international standards. To fulfill the demands of both premium and bulk industrial applications, our core operations utilize a fully computerized Automated Batch Blending (ABB) system. This guarantees exact chemical consistency, volumetric precision, and rigorous quality control across every production batch.
